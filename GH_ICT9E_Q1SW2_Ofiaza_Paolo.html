Mga Tauhan sa Noli Me Tangere

Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin

 Main character sa Noli Me Tengere. Nag- aral siya sa Espanya ng 7 years. Mukha daw siyang foreigner sabi ng mga Pilipino. Anak siya ni Rafael Ibarra.

Padre Damaso

 He is a priest that believes he is the best of the best amongst all preiest and he thinks that Filipinos shouldn't know the truth about their country.

Kapitan Tiago

 He is a wealthy man who introduced Juan Ibarra to the Filipino people.

Pilosopo Tasio

 He is a wise old man that nobody that believed in. When he died the people burned his house and his works.

Padre Salvi

 A Spanish priest who is more cunning than Padre Damaso. He assumes that he has more control over Padre Damaso's friar curate of San Diego.

Reference:

Noli Me Tangere ni Jose Rizal
Rizal J. (1887) Book: Noli Me Tangere
